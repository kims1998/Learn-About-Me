About Me

Hello, I am Charles Kim! I am 23 years old. I am a Marine Corps Veteran currently working as a On-site Quick Response Team Member. In the military I was working on the MV-22. My hobbies include going to the gym, playing video games, and now coding! I decided to become a Web Developer due to my current career coming to a halt.
